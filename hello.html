Thanks for trying 'Copy and Go (Beta)'

Welcome to the copy-and-go beta. Copy text and open links without leaving the keyboard. To get started, press Ctrl-Shift-Z to open up the copy UI. it should open up some cards with the keys you need to press in order to copy the nearby text. You can also press Ctrl-Shift-X to get a similar UI, which will alow you to open a given link in a new window.

If you have any feedback, don't hesitate to leave a review or send an email. Happy copying!
